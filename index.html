About Jennyrose

Australian artist living and working in the beautiful Eurobodalla Nature Coast of NSW. I design and make bags, jewellery, clothing and artworks from fine quality leathers, sometimes from textiles, like cashmere and merino wool.

"I like experimenting with textures & colour & incorporating unusual exotic leathers employing some original techniques. My inspiration comes from art, the various Australian landscapes from the desert to the sea and the rainforests & also fashions past and present."

I studied art & art history, sculpture, the textile crafts spinning, weaving, and printed textiles, architectural drafting and map making and masterclasses in leather craft.

I have exhibited in many group and solo exhibitions and festivals throughout Australia. I have been an Artist in residence at Uluru & designed beautiful bags incorporating opals for an opal company. I have had several galleries and since 2012 have been involved in Artisans Nest Inc. I also annually curate exhibitions at the Nest for the River of Art festival.

Special Collections

In addition to leather bags, Jennyrose Design also creates:

Handwoven Pure Cashmere

Lightweight and very warm cashmere fabric sourced directly from families of weavers in the Himalayan mountains of Ladakh, designed and handmade into original coats.

Nature Coast Leather Collection

Inspired by the 350 million year old striped sandstone domed rock formations in the world heritage listed Bungle Bungle ranges, NT Australia. Featuring Barramundi fish leather, Sea Snake and Crocodile leather.
